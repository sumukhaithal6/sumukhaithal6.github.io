Customer Feedback Analysis
Project done as an intern at Microsoft Innovation Lab, PES University. (2019)
Developed a Customer Feedback Analysis Model to analyze the customer review data of a company, and help identify critical issues based on their severity and impact on customers as well as management.
Performed Aspect Based Sentiment Analysis on the reviews to categorize the reviews and understand the sentiment of the customer. The issues are ranked based on a severity score that is generated, to finally return the top-ranked issues. The pre-processed reviews are passed to IBM’s NLU (Natural Language Understanding), a tool that offers different NLP operations.
